Experiment 1: Pathogen detector candidates

Purpose
This experiment identifies SAE latents that look like candidate pathogen detectors. It is a pre-BLAST analysis: it uses existing differential activation and k-mer enrichment outputs to rank features and attach provisional motif evidence, but it does not claim organism-level validation yet.

Inputs
- Analysis output directory resolved by METAGENIUSES_ANALYSIS_DIR or the default fallback.
- differential_feature_signature.csv: per-feature class statistics, including activation rates, mean activations, FDR values, and log odds ratios.
- kmer_enrichment.csv: enriched k-mers for each SAE feature.
- top_feature_examples.csv: example sequences that strongly activate each feature.

What it does
1. Loads differential feature statistics, k-mer enrichment results, and top activating examples.
2. Assumes 10,000 pathogen and 10,000 non-pathogen sequences, then estimates a best possible F1 score for each latent from its active/inactive counts.
3. Marks each latent as pathogen-associated, non-pathogen-associated, or not significant using FDR <= 0.05 and the sign of the pathogen/non-pathogen mean activation difference.
4. Computes log2 fold change, negative log10 FDR, and odds ratio style enrichment values.
5. Selects the top 15 pathogen-associated latents with class_enrichment >= 1.5, ranked by F1, enrichment, and mean activation difference.
6. Adds provisional labels using the top enriched k-mers for each selected latent.
7. Adds up to three top activating example sequences per selected latent, but labels their organism fields as unresolved because BLAST has not been run.

Outputs
- results/organism_detectors/volcano_plot.png: all latents plotted by pathogen/non-pathogen log2 fold change and FDR significance.
- results/organism_detectors/enrichment_histogram.png: distribution of pathogen enrichment across latents.
- results/organism_detectors/top_organism_detectors.png: bar chart of the top candidate detector latents.
- results/organism_detectors/top_detectors.csv: table of the top detector candidates and provisional motif evidence.
- results/organism_detectors/api_results.json: frontend/backend payload containing summary statistics, volcano points, top detectors, and enrichment histogram bins.

How to interpret it
A latent near the pathogen side of the volcano plot with strong FDR support and high enrichment is a candidate pathogen-associated feature. The k-mer labels are only provisional. A feature should not be described as detecting a specific organism or gene until the top activating sequences are validated with BLAST or a comparable annotation workflow.
